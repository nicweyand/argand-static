What's an Argand?

It's a way to visualize complex numbers on a 2D plane. The horizontal axis is "real," the vertical is "imaginary." That glowing dot orbits the origin. We named our search engine after it because we navigate the complex space of information to find exactly what you need.

Under the hood

Rengoku QA 🔥

Answers come with exact quotes and sources. We only extract from what's actually written—no AI making things up. Zero hallucinations.

Hybrid Search ⚡

Keyword matching + semantic understanding + 16 ranking signals. You can see exactly why each result ranked where it did. Fully transparent.

EFF Atlas 🗺️

Anti-surveillance routing using EFF's camera database. Navigate around CCTV and license plate readers. Your movement, your choice.

FoundationDB 🏛️

ACID-compliant distributed database, battle-tested and open-sourced by Apple. Serious infrastructure for serious reliability.

Rust Core 🦀

Memory-safe, blazingly fast, with no garbage collection pauses. Minimal compute, maximum power. Built to last.

Self-Hostable 🏠

Run your own instance. Entire stack is FOSS. Fork it, modify it, make it yours. No vendor lock-in whatsoever.

Argand Plane 📐

Our in-house vector database for semantic search. Built specifically for Argand. Open source like everything else.

Learning to Rank 📊

ML model that improves search quality over time. Trained on what works, not on what advertisers pay for.

DID Auth 🔐

Decentralized identity—you control your own credentials. No passwords stored on our servers. Cryptographic proof of who you are.
